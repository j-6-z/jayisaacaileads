Getting Started

A B2B prospecting platform with verified contact data for North American businesses — names, titles, direct emails, and phone numbers for decision-makers, with deep coverage of trades and service businesses. You search, filter, reveal contacts, and export lists for your outreach.

Create an account and you get 100 free credits — no credit card required. Searching and filtering is unlimited and free; credits are only spent when you reveal a contact's email. Trial credits are a one-time grant per person or company.

Data Quality

Public business sources (websites, registries, directories), licensed third-party providers, and businesses updating their own information. Every record is cross-referenced across sources and technically validated before it's marked verified. Full breakdown on our data page.

Records move through rolling re-verification cycles — emails are revalidated, dead businesses get cut, and changed roles get updated. We'd rather have a smaller accurate database than a big stale one.

Email admin@jayisaacai.com or use the contact form with the "Data removal request" topic. No account needed, no hoops. Removals are processed within 30 days — usually much faster — and stay permanent. Details in our Privacy Policy.
