Freshness

Data decays. We rebuild it on a cycle.

B2B contact data goes stale fast — people change roles, businesses close, numbers get reassigned. Records cycle through re-verification continuously instead of sitting untouched for years.

Rolling re-verification

Records are revisited on rolling monthly cycles — emails revalidated, dead businesses flagged, changed roles updated rather than left to rot.

Continuous additions

New businesses enter the database every cycle from fresh public sources and licensed feeds — newly registered companies, new locations, new decision-makers.

Aggressive pruning

A smaller, accurate database beats a bloated stale one. Bounced emails, disconnected numbers, and closed businesses get cut — they don't pad our contact count.

Verification

How a record earns its place

01

Sourcing

Records are compiled from public business sources — websites, registries, directories — plus licensed third-party data and businesses updating their own listings.

02

Cross-referencing

Every record is matched across multiple sources. Conflicting data gets resolved or flagged; single-source records carry lower confidence until corroborated.

03

Technical validation

Emails are checked against live mail servers for deliverability. Phone numbers are validated for format, region, and line status before a record is marked verified.

04

Suppression screening

Records are screened against our opt-out suppression list. Anyone who has requested removal stays removed — permanently.

The honest caveat

No B2B database on earth is 100% accurate — anyone claiming otherwise is selling you something. People change jobs mid-cycle and businesses close overnight. Our commitment: verified-marked records meet our validation bar at time of verification, stale data gets cut fast, and if you hit a bad batch, tell us and we'll make it right.
