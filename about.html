Our Story

Built for the trades. Not Silicon Valley.

JAYISAAC AI started with a simple frustration: the big contact databases are priced for enterprise teams and built around tech companies. If you sell to plumbers, HVAC shops, roofers, electricians, and contractors — the businesses that actually answer their phones — the data is thin, stale, or locked behind a five-figure annual contract.

So we built our own. JAYISAAC AI is a prospecting platform with verified contact data for North American businesses, with deep coverage of the trades and local service economy that other platforms treat as an afterthought.

We're founder-built and independent. Every feature ships because a real sales workflow needed it — not because a product committee approved it. That means fast iteration, straight answers, and pricing a small team can actually afford.

What We Believe

The principles behind the product

Data you can dial

A contact record is only worth something if the phone rings and the email lands. We prioritize verification and freshness over inflating our record count.

Pricing without the ambush

No forced annual contracts, no "talk to sales" wall, no surprise overage invoices. Plain plans, transparent credits, cancel whenever.

Privacy isn't a footnote

We operate under PIPEDA and honor removal requests fast. Business contact data, handled like it belongs to real people — because it does.

AI that does work, not demos

We use AI where it saves you hours — enrichment, list building, personalization — not as a buzzword to pad a pitch deck.
