Straight answers

The questions security people actually ask.

Is this replacing my security team?

No, and anyone selling you that is lying. It replaces the parts of the job nobody wants: watching a dashboard at 3am, triaging the same duplicate alert forty times, and writing up an incident you've already written up. The judgment calls stay with your people.

What happens when it isn't sure?

It escalates. AI contains what it's confident about and pages a human for anything ambiguous. We'd rather wake you for a real question than quietly auto-remediate something we read wrong.

Can I run it without letting it touch anything?

Yes. Start in read-only. It monitors, correlates, and alerts, and takes zero action until you switch playbooks on — one at a time, on your schedule.

Do I need a security background to run it?

No. Findings come in plain English with the recommended action attached, ranked by what's actually exploitable. If you can read your inbox, you can run this.

What does it cost?

Flat monthly pricing. No per-seat fees, no per-alert billing, no annual lock-in, and no "contact sales" wall to find out the number. See pricing.

What if it misses something?

No tool catches everything, and we won't claim otherwise. What we will do is keep a full timeline of every event and decision, so when you review an incident you get the actual record instead of a shrug.
